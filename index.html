About Me!

Bio

  • I am originally from a small border town in Arizona called Yuma, and proud of it! I love the desert. I have been married for four years to my wife, Taylor. We are expecting our first child in February.

Education


  • I attended college at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. I always planned on being an active duty Army officer, so I chose history as a major because it aligned with my interests and would allow me to quickly begin my Army career.

Job Experience

  • I worked as a Pricing Analyst at Zillow Offers, then moved into an analytical/account management role in Renovations. I also have worked as an light infantry and mechanized infantry platoon leader in the SC and AZ Army National Guard.

Goals

  • I will become a software engineer at Zillow. Eventually, I would like to experience more of what the software engineering world has to offer, such as roles in product management or life at a startup.
